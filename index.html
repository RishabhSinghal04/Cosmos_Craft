About This Site

Space Journey is a simple static website created to celebrate the beauty and history of space exploration. The Gallery showcases stunning visuals of planets, galaxies, and spacecraft, while the Missions section highlights key milestones in astronomy and human spaceflight.

Whether you're here to admire the universe's wonders or to learn about historic missions, this site is your gateway to the stars.
